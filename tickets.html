Tickets for the festival are affordable and accessible to all visitors. Guests can choose between single-day tickets or full packages. Registration can be done online or at the venue. Early booking is recommended.

Tickets include access to all activities and guided tours. Discounts are available for students and groups. The process is simple and efficient. Visitors must keep tickets safe for entry.
